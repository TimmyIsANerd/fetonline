FAQs

We offer both couple classes and private lessons. Couple classes (up to 2 students) are held live, and meet at a set time weekly for up to 12 weeks. Group classes are recorded, and students have access to the lesson recordings for the duration of the course. Private lessons are tailored to each student’s goals and French level. There is no set start or end time, students simply book whatever times are convenient for them. You may book up to 32 private lessons at once. Lesson packages are also available

We work with students ages 10 and up

Yes, you can schedule a lesson with a spouse, partner, family member, friend - whoever you want.

Adult courses and private lessons are 55 minutes long.

Lessons are conducted via Zoom, a platform that is free to learners.

We have language tutors who have B.Sc or B.Ed in the language, have teaching experience (virtual or physical) of more than 4 years, and have taken professional examinations in the language.
